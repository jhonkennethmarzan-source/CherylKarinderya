Our Story

Che Karinderya started on 2025, with one simple mission: to serve honest, home-cooked Filipino meals that everyone loves.

What began as a small passion project among a group of mothers has grown into a beloved neighborhood eatery. Every dish is prepared fresh daily using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

We believe in keeping things simple, genuine, and full of flavor—just like the meals you'd find at a mother’s table. Our kitchen is run by the same community of cooks that started it all, ensuring every plate is made with care and love. We take special pride in introducing our heirloom dishes to new friends.

Whether you're a regular or visiting for the first time, you're family here. Kain na tayo!
